     The new regulations on the retirement years of motor vehicles, for the delay of the car scrap age procedures, the owner needs to carry the relevant application materials to the vehicle management office, the necessary procedures are mainly the vehicle owner's identification, motor vehicle driving license, motor vehicle registration certificate, motor vehicle third-form liability compulsory insurance certificate, motor vehicle test form.
     After the owner submits the application, the car management office will, according to the application materials, examine and test the owner and the vehicle that applies to delay the retirement life of the vehicle, and if the situation meets the requirements, the car management office will issue a certificate approving the delay of the retirement age of the car.
